About The Event

The CSE Scientific Club of the National School Supérieure of computer
science (ESI) delighted to present to you a preview of “Green Hack”
a revolutionary event that combines ingenuity technological
and ecological commitment.

CSE -Club Scientifique de l'ESI- is a student club at the
Higher National School of Computer Science, Algiers.
It is created for computer science, high technology,
robotics, design and anything else technology-related
enthusiasts. CSE's activities made it establish itself on
a national & international scale and became one of the country's
largest & most active clubs.
